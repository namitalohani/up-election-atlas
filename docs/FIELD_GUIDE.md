# Field Guide

## swing_seat

Definition:
A constituency that has changed the winning party in at least two of the last three Assembly elections.

Example:
Lucknow Central

---

## turnout_pct

Definition:
Percentage of registered electors who cast a vote.

Source:
Election Commission of India

---

## first_time_voters

Definition:
Electors voting for the first time in an election, generally aged 18–19, as reported by the Election Commission.

Do not estimate this value.
