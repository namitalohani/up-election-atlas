Every constituency follows the same pipeline.

## Step 1

Create constituency folder.

Example

data/constituencies/174-lucknow-central/

---

## Step 2

Collect official sources.

Priority

ECI

ADR

Census

Government

---

## Step 3

Generate structured JSON using the official schema.

---

## Step 4

Run validation.

- JSON validation
- Required fields
- Source verification

---

## Step 5

Human review.

No AI-generated file should be merged without manual review.

---

## Step 6

Commit.

---

## Step 7

Publish.

The constituency becomes part of the public dataset.
