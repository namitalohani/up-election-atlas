# Source Hierarchy

Tier 1 (Highest Trust)

- Election Commission of India
- Census of India
- Government of Uttar Pradesh
- Parliament / Vidhan Sabha

Tier 2

- ADR / MyNeta
- PRS
- RBI
- NITI Aayog

Tier 3

- CSDS
- IndiaStat
- Government dashboards

Tier 4

- Indian Express
- The Hindu
- Times of India
- Hindustan Times

Tier 5

- Regional newspapers

Tier 6

- Wikipedia

Wikipedia should never be the final source if an official source exists.
